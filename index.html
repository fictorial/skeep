SKEEP

  • How to Play SKEEP

    The goal is to be the first to play all the cards in your stock pile. Players take turns trying to build sequences of cards from 1-12 in the 4 build piles by moving cards from your stock, one of the discard piles, or your hand.

    When no moves remain, discard a card from your hand to one of 4 discard piles. If you play all 5 cards in your hand, you will be drawn another 5 immediately.

    W cards are wild. Discard piles do not require a sequence.

    To move a card, select the card and then select the target card pile. To deselect a selected card, select the card again.
